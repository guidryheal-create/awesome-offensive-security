# Learning Platforms & Courses

- [NetworkChuck Academy](https://academy.networkchuck.com/courses) - NetworkChuck Academy provides free and accessible cybersecurity courses for beginners.
- [Just Hacking](https://www.justhacking.com/types/courses-and-paths/) - Just Hacking offers curated courses and learning paths for ethical hacking and cybersecurity.
